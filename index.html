Safety & Compliance

Trans Amadi Logistics Services maintains full compliance with Nigerian safety regulations and international logistics standards. Our drivers complete monthly safety training, all trucks carry fire suppression systems and emergency kits, and loads are secured per FRSC guidelines with certified tie-downs.

We maintain DPR certification for petroleum product haulage and comprehensive third-party liability insurance. Our safety record speaks volumes: zero fatalities since 2015, FIRS-compliant tax documentation, and we don't just meet standards—we set them for Rivers State logistics operations.
